# Healthcare Claims Platform

## Domain
Healthcare revenue cycle management focused on claims submission,
denial analysis, eligibility verification, and prior authorization.

## Problem
Providers struggle with denied claims and slow reimbursements.

## Solution
An AI-driven platform that analyzes claims, predicts denials,
and optimizes submission workflows.
